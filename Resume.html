About.

Radio and television broadcaster Dave Wilson is a familiar play-by-play
voice on college, pro and high school sports coverage in and around
Northeast Ohio. Whatever the venue or sport he covers, Wilson brings a
versatile resume of broadcasting experience.

A long-time resident of Chagrin Falls, Ohio, Wilson has built an
impressive career calling a myriad of sports on radio and television over
the years.
